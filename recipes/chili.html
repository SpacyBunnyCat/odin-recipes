Chili

Description

Wonderful meal for a winter's day

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 package chili seasoning
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 can kidney beans - drained
  • 1 can chili beans
  • salt & pepper
  • 1 onion - diced

Steps

  1. Brown meat in ductch oven or large pot on medium-high heat. Drain fat. Set aside.
  2. Add onion to a small amount of the beef fat. Cook until translucent.
  3. Add seasoning packet, all tomatoes (diced, sauce, and paste). Stir to combine.
  4. Add in both cans of beans. Stir. Add back in the beef.
  5. Heat to boiling. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste. Now, you can eat now or allow to simmer until you are ready (up to 2 hours).
